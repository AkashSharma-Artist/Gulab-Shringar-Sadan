Our Story

Two shops, side by side, one family

Gulab Shringar Sadan began as a single shringar counter in Utai and grew into two shops standing side by side — one dedicated entirely to bridal and everyday shringar, the other to gifting for every occasion life brings.

Whether you're shopping for a wedding trousseau, a festival's worth of bangles, or a last-minute birthday gift for someone you love, you'll find it here — picked out personally, wrapped with care, and always with a smile.
